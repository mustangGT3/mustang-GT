5.2 ‘Voodoo’ (V8) – The High-Reving Shelby Beast

The 5.2-liter Voodoo V8 is Ford’s high-performance masterpiece, powering Shelby GT350 and GT350R Mustangs from 2015 to 2020. Famous for its flat-plane crank design, this engine delivers unmatched high-revving performance and precision, making it a standout among modern American muscle engines.

Key Highlights:

  • Displacement: 5.2 liters (V8)
  • Configuration: Flat-plane crank V8
  • Power Output: Approximately 526 hp
  • Torque: Around 429 lb-ft, delivered across a broad rev range
  • Redline: Exceptionally high at 8,250 RPM, perfect for track-focused driving

Features and Performance:

Unlike traditional cross-plane V8s, the Voodoo engine uses a flat-plane crank, allowing it to rev higher and produce a sharper, more exotic exhaust note reminiscent of European supercars. Its high-revving character and precision engineering provide incredible responsiveness, making it ideal for track performance and spirited driving.

The Voodoo also features aluminum block and heads, advanced intake design, and efficient airflow, ensuring optimal power and reliability under extreme conditions. Enthusiasts praise its instant throttle response, rev-happy nature, and aggressive soundtrack—a true driver’s engine.

Legacy:

The 5.2 Voodoo engine represents Ford’s commitment to engineering excellence and performance innovation. It turned the Shelby GT350 into a world-class track-capable machine while staying road-friendly. Its flat-plane design, extreme revving potential, and thrilling driving experience have earned it a legendary status among muscle car enthusiasts, making it one of the most celebrated modern V8s in Mustang history.
