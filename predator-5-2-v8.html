5.2 ‘Predator’ (V8) – The Modern Supercharged Muscle Monster

The 5.2-liter Predator V8 is Ford’s latest high-performance powerhouse, debuting in 2022 in top-tier Shelby GT500 and other high-performance Mustang models. This engine represents the next evolution of American muscle, combining massive power with cutting-edge engineering.

Key Highlights:

  • Displacement: 5.2 liters (V8)
  • Configuration: Supercharged V8
  • Power Output: Over 760 hp in GT500 variants
  • Torque: Around 625 lb-ft, delivering brutal acceleration
  • Performance Focus: Track-capable, drag-strip ready, extreme high-speed performance

Features and Performance:

The Predator engine builds on the legacy of Ford’s 5.0 and 5.2 V8s but adds a modern supercharger system, advanced cooling, and reinforced internals to handle extreme power. Unlike naturally aspirated engines, the supercharged Predator delivers instant, colossal torque, making 0–60 mph times blisteringly fast.

Its design emphasizes high durability, track-ready performance, and technological sophistication, with features like forged internals, high-flow heads, and optimized airflow. The engine’s ferocious power and responsiveness give the latest Shelby and GT500-level models an unmatched driving experience.

Legacy:

Though brand-new, the 5.2 Predator is already cementing itself as a modern muscle legend. It pushes the boundaries of what an American V8 can achieve, offering supercar-level performance while retaining Mustang DNA. With its raw power, advanced engineering, and track dominance, the Predator defines the next generation of high-performance V8 engines.
