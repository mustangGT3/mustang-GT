Mustang Classic

The original pony car — iconic styling and driving character from the 1960s that inspired generations.

Key Specs

  • Era: 1964 - 1973 (first generation)
  • Engine: Small-block V8 options (Popular 289ci, 302ci)
  • Power: 200–400+ HP (dependent on options & tuning)
  • Layout: FR layout (front-engine, rear-wheel drive)
  • Body styles: Coupe, Fastback, Convertible

History & Significance

The Mustang was launched in 1964 and immediately captured customer attention with its sporty design and accessible price. The Classic Mustang became a cultural icon and defined the 'pony car' segment — long hoods, short rear decks, and a focus on fun-to-drive dynamics. Collectors prize early Mustangs for their design purity and historical importance.

Restoration & Ownership Tips

If you're restoring a Classic, focus on rust repair, correct suspension bushings, and sourcing correct parts. Authenticity matters for value; however, sympathetic upgrades (brakes, cooling) improve usability while preserving character.
