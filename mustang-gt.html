Mustang GT

The modern performance coupe — powerful V8s, sharp handling, and a legacy of speed.

Key Specs

  • Engine: 5.0L V8 (GT), 2.3L EcoBoost options
  • Power: 450+ HP (depending on tune)
  • Transmission: 6-speed manual / 10-speed automatic
  • 0-60mph: ~4.0s (model and conditions dependent)
  • Drive: Rear-wheel drive

Performance & Upgrades

Popular upgrades include cold-air intakes, ECU remaps, performance exhausts, suspension upgrades, and brake packages. Track-focused builds may add roll cages, tuned differentials, and lightweight components.
